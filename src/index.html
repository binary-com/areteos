How Areteos Coin works
The value of Areteos Coin is pegged to an asset with real value. In our case, Areteos Coin is pegged to the US dollar. This means that 1 Areteos Coin will always be worth 1 US dollar.
Pegged to US dollar

Areteos Coin is a stablecoin pegged to the US dollar on a 1-to-1 basis. It is issued by Areteos Coins Limited, and backed by the Global Group.
